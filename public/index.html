How to play 🐄

Goal. Everyone answers the same question on their own phone. You score when your answer matches the majority — the herd.

Scoring. Match the biggest group → +1 point. A tie or no majority → nobody scores.

The Pink Cow 🐮. Give an answer nobody else matched and you’re stuck with the Pink Cow. It passes on when someone else later goes solo. You can’t win while holding it.

Winning. First to 8 points wins — unless you’re holding the cow, then you have to wait.

The leader 👑. Whoever creates the room runs each round: start, reveal, merge duplicate answers, score, next.

Tip: think boring. The obvious answer is usually the safe one.
